The Baddest Ari Music Career

The Baddest Ari got her start in the music industry by winning a Beat making competition in Miami, Fl when she was only 16. Making her the youngest winner to ever win this competition. The prize for winning this competition was a record deal with Dream World records. Not even a month later she came out with her first big hit The ECHO.

With The ECHO putting all eyes on her she came out with her first album, The Baddest. In the first week it debuted at number 1 on the charts, and it went double platinum. She became the youngest to ever achieve theses accomplishments.

With all eyes on her she is currently working hard on her second album.
