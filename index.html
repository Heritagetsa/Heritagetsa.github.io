What Is TSA?

"TSA (Technology Student Association) is a national organization comprised of students devoted to science, technology, engineering, & mathematics. TSA has over 250,000 members in middle & high school across the United States. Said members learn through projects, competitive events, leadership opportunities, & much more. This influence from TSA will bring what is needed for a technological erudite society."
